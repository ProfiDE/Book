# The Parrot

Today, I went to the park. There, I saw a parrot sitting on a branch. It was a free parrot—not in a cage.

Suddenly, we started talking. We spent a nice morning together. It told me about the sky, and I listened with a smile.

The sun was warm on my face. Birds sang all around us. Then the parrot spread its wings and flew to a taller tree.

I waved and said goodbye. It was a morning I will never forget.

It was a fictional story for testing Git :)
